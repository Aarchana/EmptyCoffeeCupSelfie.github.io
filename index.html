   Empty Coffee Cup Selfie Challenge - Shun a Few Extra Calories and help a 22 yr old save her 52 yr old mother - April 27, 2017.

Inspired by Ice Bucket Challenge and No Makeup Selfie Challenge which reassured us about women power worldwide, we dare you to take Empty Coffee Cup Selfie Challenge (#EmptyCoffeeCupSelfie) - donate a cup of coffee and shun a few extra calories and help us raise funds for medical treatment of our colleague's mother who is suffering from cancer in food pipe.

As per internet, a cup of coffee may have:

               Calories   Fat (g)
Caffè Latte     190         7
Caffè Mocha     290         8
Cappuccino      120         4

Let go a cup of coffee this morning and donate the same or half the money at least ($1 or 50 Indian Rupees) to this campaign. Please visit our fundraising partner Ketto for online donations @https://www.ketto.org/emptycoffeecupselfie


If you are still reading, here’s a brief into our colleague's world:

It's 2 of them who have been on their own for last 5-6 years supporting each other and dreaming to make it big. That’s their small world. Yes, it’s she(22) and her mother(52).

They were progressing quite well in their pursuit to have a good life. The mother supported the daughter throughout her days in Engineering College (4 yrs). The daughter started her career very recently and as she was making her mother proud with her achievements, they figured out, the mother won’t be there for long, if not treated in a good medical facility.

Her mother got diagnosed with cancer in food pipe (advanced stage). The daughter started her treatment by raising some funds with help from her colleagues. But as we all know, treatment for Cancer has a big cost and the daughter burned out the initial funding quite fast due to a costly operation. The daughter is again in need of funds of the tune of $18750 or 1200000 Indian Rupees to continue her treatment.

Live and stay healthy, donate a cup of coffee today ($1 or 50 Indian Rupees) to this campaign. Please visit our fundraising partner Ketto for online donations @https://www.ketto.org/emptycoffeecupselfie
